The Process

How MediAI Works

From symptom selection to personalised precautions — here's exactly what happens.

01

Select Your Symptoms

Choose from 132 clinically validated binary symptom indicators. You can search, filter by category (Fever, Pain, Skin, Gastro, Neuro, Respiratory), or scroll through the full list.

02

ML Inference

Your symptom vector passes through a StandardScaler normalisation, then a Logistic Regression classifier trained on 3,936 patient records with 5-fold cross-validation and GridSearchCV tuning.

03

Ranked Diagnoses

Top-5 differential diagnoses are returned with probability percentages, a confidence bar, and a Low / Medium / High risk badge based on prediction strength.

04

Personalised Precautions

For every predicted disease, MediAI provides a tailored precaution guide — what to do, what to avoid, and which emergency signs mean you should call a doctor immediately.
