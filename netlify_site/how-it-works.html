The Process

How MediAI Works

From symptom selection to personalised precautions — here’s exactly what happens.

01

Select Your Symptoms

Choose from 132 clinically validated binary symptom indicators. Search, filter by category, or scroll through the full list.

02

ML Inference

Your symptom vector passes through a StandardScaler normalisation, then a Logistic Regression classifier trained on 3,936 patient records with 5-fold cross-validation.

03

Ranked Diagnoses

Top-5 differential diagnoses returned with probability percentages, a confidence bar, and Low / Medium / High risk badge.

04

Personalised Precautions

For every predicted disease, MediAI provides a tailored guide — what to do, what to avoid, and emergency signs to watch for.
